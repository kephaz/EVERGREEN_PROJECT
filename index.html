About Us

Welcome to EPS

Evergreen Protective technologies (EPS) is a full protective technologies company duly incorporated to provide professional security technologies in the USA and Nigeria West Africa. We pride on our leaderships’ innovation, integrity and professionalism which collectively spans of 90years of security expertise. We are equipped and committed to protect your assets effectively better than any other security service providers. At EPS, we understand that security is an ever-evolving challenge. That’s why our security professionals stay ahead of the curve with industry-leading technology, innovative system design, and world-class project management

Based on our antecedent in provision of SMART security technologies both in the US and Africa. We are elated to partner with you in offering the best innovative security solutions.
